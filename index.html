Research Publications

Optimizing and Analysing the Crater Wear in CNC Machining using Taguchi Optimization Technique
Dhruv Bhatia, Rishab Jain, Roop Lal
International Journal of Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (IJAPIE)
Vol 6, Issue 4, 2021 | Pages: 19-30 | DOI: 10.35121/ijapie202110453
Abstract: Flank wear and crater wear are the most important factors that affect tool life and tribological properties of workpiece. This study optimizes crater wear to smallest value using Taguchi optimization analysis. Experiments were conducted on CNC machining with aluminum Al7075 as base material. Parameters studied included feed, speed, and depth of cut. Results show that speed is the most prominent factor in reducing crater wear followed by feed and depth of cut.

Optimizing and Analysing the Flank Wear for Turning an Aluminum Alloy using Taguchi Optimization Technique
Rishab Jain, Ramakant Rana, Ashwni
International Journal of Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (IJAPIE)
Vol 6, Issue 2, 2021 | Pages: 10-20 | DOI: 10.35121/ijapie202104252
Abstract: Aluminum is light in weight and strong which makes it a good material in manufacturing. This paper analyzes and calculates flank wear of tool bits used for machining aluminum Al7075. Experiments were done on a CNC turning center. Taguchi analysis was used to analyze different machining parameters like feed, speed, and depth of cut. Results found that feed plays an important role in decreasing flank wear during CNC machining.
